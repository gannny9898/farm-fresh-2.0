Frequently Asked Questions

How does Farm Fresh ensure product quality?

We work directly with vetted farmers who follow organic and sustainable practices. All products undergo quality checks before being delivered to ensure you receive only the freshest items.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to major cities and surrounding areas. Enter your pincode during checkout to check if we deliver to your location.

How do I become a supplier?

If you're a farmer interested in joining our platform, please register as a farmer and our team will reach out to you for verification and onboarding.
